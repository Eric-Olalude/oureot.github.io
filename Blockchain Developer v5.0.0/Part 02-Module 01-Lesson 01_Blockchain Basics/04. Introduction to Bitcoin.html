04. Introduction to Bitcoin

Introduction to Bitcoin

The blockchain is a huge topic spanning many platforms and industries. With so much to learn and new updates happening everyday it's tough to know where to start. We believe the best place to start is where it all started - with Bitcoin!

The concepts and ideas bitcoin developed continue to influence all other blockchains. For that reason, we'll use bitcoin as a way to help walk through some important concepts in the program.

In this section, we'll be starting off by going over the basics of Bitcoin!

What is Bitcoin?

In this first video, we'll define what Bitcoin is and how it started. We'll also look at how it's core ideas helped establish what we know today as the blockchain.

Review the Bitcoin Whitepaper

Many of the concepts that Nakamoto wrote about - from building in privacy to ensuring the system grows - extend beyond Bitcoin and even beyond just cryptocurrency. These are concepts that are fundamental to building a robust blockchain for any application.

For this task, review the Bitcoin white paper. Reflect on what problem with the existing system that Bitcoin aimed to solve and the proposed solution. It’s expected that there you will encounter terms and concepts that aren’t familiar yet. It’s okay. We will cover these in the program. For now, try to understand the issue at a high-level.
