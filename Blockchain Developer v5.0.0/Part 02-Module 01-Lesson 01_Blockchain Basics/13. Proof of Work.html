13. Proof of Work

Proof of Work

One of the first algorithms created for consensus is known as proof of work. The idea behind proof of work is that whoever puts in the most work to contribute to the system is the most trustworthy.

In this section, we'll go over the details of what proof of work is, why it's important, and some of the pros and cons of using this algorithm to drive consensus for a blockchain.

Problems with Proof of Work

Proof of work was the first implementation of a blockchain consensus algorithm, originally used by Bitcoin. Like most first attempts, we are noticing some problems with it and some ways it can be improved.

Coming up, we'll discuss some of these problems.
