04. Ethereum Tools and Community

Ethereum Tools and Community

While making Ethereum decentralized applications, you'll encounter many new tools and communities. These will become an invaluable resource as you continue growing as an Ethereum Blockchain Developer. Here you'll see ways to get connected with this amazing network of tools and communities for yourself.

Objectives

Our goal here is to help you do the following things.

  1. Describe the basics of tools you'll work with as you build Ethereum applications.
  2. Join the core Ethereum Blockchain Developer communities

2. Ethereum Communities

Ethereum has an extremely helpful and vibrant community of developers!

Connecting with this community is a huge part of the Ethereum Blockchain Developer learning experience. Participating in these communities will take you a long way toward becoming a successful Ethereum developer.

5.2-Ethereum-Communities

As shown in the video, a great place to find Ethereum communities is on their home page. Go to Ethereum.org and scroll to the bottom of the page.

Here's a list of some of the community resources I find useful. Take some time to look through them and join a few of the communities when you get the chance.
