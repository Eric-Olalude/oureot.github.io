3. Ethereum Website

Now that you've been introduced to Ethereum, feel free to check out ethereum.org.

This is the Ethereum home page and it has many interesting details to help get you started with Ethereum. If something isn't quite familiar to you yet, that's fine, you'll hear all about it in the upcoming lessons!
