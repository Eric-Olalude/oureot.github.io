01. Intro to Supply Chain Management

Intro To Supply Chain Management

This is already starting at a large scale. In early 2018, Maersk, the world's largest container shipping company, and IBM partnered to create TradeLens, a platform to build open standards for applying blockchain to the world’s global supply chain.

TradeLens uses IBM Blockchain technology as the foundation for digital supply chains, allowing multiple trading partners to collaborate by establishing a single shared view of a transaction without compromising detail and privacy.

Maersk and IBM were the first to join TradeLens, but more than 90 organizations around the world have joined in to participate in the TradeLens platform.
