# Proof of Existence

We just learned how proof of existence is used to verify and protect intellectual property whether that's a documents, media file, or another piece of digital data. Proof of existence relies on timestamped transactions on the blockchain. Let's hear from Jarrod Dicker, CEO of Po.et which is decentralized protocol for content ownership, discovery
and monetization in media.
