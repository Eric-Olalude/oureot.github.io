03. Blockchain Identity

Blockchain Identity

Welcome to this section on blockchain identity! Like a social security number, a bank account, a drivers license, or a club membership, your blockchain identity is how you establish yourself in the world, in this case the blockchain world.

In this section, we'll go over the basics of blockchain identities. You'll hear many new terms and ideas that will help set you up to create and manage your own personal blockchain identity.

Blockchain Identity Overview

In this first video, we'll cover things like wallet addresses, private keys, public keys, and more to learn what they are and how they contribute to creating your blockchain identity.

Blockchain Identity

Blockchain Hashing Algorithms

With a better understanding of the goals and purpose of wallet addresses, private keys, and public keys, we can now look further into how each of these are created.

By using hashing algorithms, blockchains help create our identity in a way that is extremely secure.

In the next video, we'll dive deeper into each of these algorithms to understand how the blockchain ensures the security of our addresses and keys.

Blockchain Identity Part 2

Key Terms

Wallet Address: A unique identifier for your wallet

Private Key: A secret number that allows you to spend bitcoin from your wallet.

Public Key: Publicly shareable key that cannot be used to spend bitcoin.
